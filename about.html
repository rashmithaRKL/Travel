About Sri Lanka

Discover the magic of this tropical paradise

Welcome to Paradise

Sri Lanka, formerly known as Ceylon, is a jewel-shaped island nation in South Asia. With a history spanning over 3,000 years, this tropical paradise offers visitors an incredible array of experiences, from ancient ruins to pristine beaches, from misty mountains to vibrant wildlife.

Rich Cultural Heritage

Home to eight UNESCO World Heritage sites, Sri Lanka boasts a rich cultural heritage that spans millennia. From the ancient cities of Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa to the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic in Kandy, the island's cultural treasures offer visitors a glimpse into its fascinating past.

Natural Beauty

From pristine beaches along its coast to misty mountains in the central highlands, Sri Lanka's landscapes are incredibly diverse. The country is home to lush rainforests, cascading waterfalls, and sprawling tea plantations that paint the hills in sheets of green.

Diverse Wildlife

Sri Lanka is one of the best places in Asia for wildlife viewing. The country's national parks are home to elephants, leopards, sloth bears, and countless bird species. The waters around the island also host blue whales, sperm whales, and several species of dolphins.

Culinary Delights

Sri Lankan cuisine is a reflection of its history and cultural diversity. Known for its liberal use of spices, fresh ingredients, and unique cooking methods, the local food offers a gastronomic adventure that's not to be missed.
